Welcome to Daypart Analyzer
Understand exactly when your Amazon Sponsored Products ads perform best — and when they're wasting budget or going dark. Upload two reports to get started.
1
SP Budget Report — In Amazon Ads: Reports → Sponsored Products → Budget → Daily · Last 7 days · Download CSV
2
SP Campaign Report — Same location → Campaign → Hourly · Last 7 days · Download CSV
3
Drop both files into the upload zones on the left and right, then click Analyze →
Note: All data is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
SB / SD campaigns: This tool currently supports Sponsored Products only. Sponsored Brands and Display support is coming soon.
Day Part Analysis
See how your account or campaigns perform hour-by-hour — averaged across your selected date range. Use this to identify your best and worst windows for spend efficiency.
📊
Summary Strip
Total Spend, Sales, ROAS, and Orders for the selected period — with per-day averages beneath each.
📅
Day Selector
Filter to specific days of the week. Select multiple to average them together, or use All 7 Days for the full picture.
📈
4 Hourly Charts
Avg Spend, Sales, ROAS, and Orders plotted across all 24 hours. Bars are heat-colored green→red by relative value.
🔍
Click Any Bar
Click a bar to drill into that hour — see every campaign ranked by sales, spend, ROAS, or orders for that specific hour.
Tip: The ROAS chart uses a purple line. Hours with high ROAS but low spend are under-invested — consider increasing bids in those windows.
Expand: Click the ⤢ icon on any chart to open it fullscreen for a closer look.
By Campaign / Category
Switch from account-level averages to a custom selection of campaigns. Useful for isolating a product line, keyword type, or targeting strategy.
1
Search — Type any keyword to find campaigns. Results appear as chips — click to select. Multiple selections are averaged together.
2
Quick-select — Use + Branded or + Non-Branded to instantly select all | B | or | NB | campaigns.
3
Portfolio dropdown — Select all campaigns from a portfolio in one click. Auto-detected from the budget report.
4
Browse all — Opens the full campaign list. Use Select all (N) to bulk-select everything matching your current search.
Selected campaigns appear as green chips above the search bar with an × to remove individually.
Compare View
Side-by-side hourly comparison between two groups of campaigns. Auto-populates Branded vs Non-Branded on first load — fully customizable from there.
🟢
Group A — Branded
Auto-fills with campaigns containing | B |. Rename, add, or remove campaigns freely. Stats shown in green.
🔵
Group B — Non-Branded
Auto-fills with | NB | campaigns. Stats shown in blue. Each group has its own campaign count and totals.
📊
4 Overlaid Charts
Spend, Sales, ROAS, and Orders — both groups on each chart. Hover any hour to see both values and the difference.
📐
ROAS Dual Axes
Branded and non-branded ROAS often differ wildly. Each group gets its own Y-axis so both lines are readable.
Custom groups: You can rename either group and use the search to add any campaign — great for comparing product lines like Bags vs Towels.
Uncategorized tray: Campaigns that don't match | B | or | NB | appear below with →A and →B buttons to assign them.
Out of Budget
Identifies which campaigns exhausted their daily budget over the last 7 days — and shows the exact hour they went dark each day.
🔴
OOB Threshold
A campaign is flagged as Out of Budget when it spends ≥ 95% of its daily budget. Sorted by frequency.
📅
7-Day Calendar
Each day shown as a tile — red for OOB, amber for near-budget (90–95%), green for healthy. Shows % of budget used.
⏱️
Hourly Spend Curve
Heat-colored bars show spend per hour. The yellow line is cumulative spend. Red dashed line is the daily budget ceiling.
🗓️
Avg Day View
Shows the average spend pattern across all 7 days, with a vertical marker showing the typical hour budget runs out.
Action: If a campaign hits budget before 8PM, consider increasing the daily budget or reducing bids during low-ROAS hours to extend reach.
Heatmap: The grid at the bottom shows spend intensity by hour across all 7 days — hover any cell for exact values.
